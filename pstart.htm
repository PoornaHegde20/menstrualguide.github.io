How do you know when you will get your first period?



Menstruation is a part of puberty. It's normal, healthy and a positive part of growing up! If you are experiencing any of the following changes, chances are your first period is probably going to come soon!
  • Your breasts start to grow
  • You become taller
  • Your face/hair/body may become oilier
  • You begin to grow underarm, legs and pubic hair
  • Your body starts to develop its own odour
  • You start to notice some whitish fluid traces in your panties
  • You'll experience new emotions and feelings
